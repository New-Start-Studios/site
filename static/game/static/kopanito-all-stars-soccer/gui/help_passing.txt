<p>There are a few ways to pass the ball between players: a direct pass, a lob pass and a through-ball pass.</p>

<h3>Direct Pass</h3>
<figure>
    <img src="images/help/direct_pass.png">
    <figcaption>
        <p>Tap very quickly <span class="key auto-wide auto-update" data-action="pass">S</span></p>
    </figcaption>
</figure>
<p>To pass directly to one of your teammates, aim with
    <span class="grid middle">
        <span class="key auto-wide auto-update" data-action="up"></span>
        <span class="key auto-wide auto-update" data-action="down"></span>
        <span class="key auto-wide auto-update" data-action="left"></span>
        <span class="key auto-wide auto-update" data-action="right"></span>
    </span> in the direction of the receiving player and tap very quickly <span class="key auto-wide auto-update" data-action="pass">S</span>.</p>

<h3>Through Ball</h3>
<figure>
    <img src="images/help/through_ball.png">
    <figcaption>
        <p>Tap <span class="key auto-wide auto-update" data-action="pass">S</span> slightly longer</p>
    </figcaption>
</figure>
<p>If you press <span class="key auto-wide auto-update" data-action="pass">S</span> a little longer, ball will be kicked in a straight line in the direction pointed by
    <span class="grid middle">
        <span class="key auto-wide auto-update" data-action="up"></span>
        <span class="key auto-wide auto-update" data-action="down"></span>
        <span class="key auto-wide auto-update" data-action="left"></span>
        <span class="key auto-wide auto-update" data-action="right"></span>
    </span>. Use this to send the ball between opponents or to perform more advanced tactics.</p>

<h3>Lob pass</h3>
<figure>
    <img src="images/help/lob_pass.png">
    <figcaption>
        <p>Hold <span class="key auto-wide auto-update" data-action="lob">A</span></p>
    </figcaption>
</figure>
<p>To make the lob pass, hold <span class="key auto-wide auto-update" data-action="lob">A</span> and use
    <span class="grid middle">
        <span class="key auto-wide auto-update" data-action="up"></span>
        <span class="key auto-wide auto-update" data-action="down"></span>
        <span class="key auto-wide auto-update" data-action="left"></span>
        <span class="key auto-wide auto-update" data-action="right"></span>
    </span>
    to indicate direction in which you want to kick the ball. The longer you hold, the further the ball will go.</p>
<p>Passes and lobs can be chained, e.g. you can perform them just before your player receives the ball, but itâ€™s required to hold proper direction (
    <span class="grid middle">
        <span class="key auto-wide auto-update" data-action="up"></span>
        <span class="key auto-wide auto-update" data-action="down"></span>
        <span class="key auto-wide auto-update" data-action="left"></span>
        <span class="key auto-wide auto-update" data-action="right"></span>
    </span>
    ) to the moment when player starts kicking the ball.</p>
